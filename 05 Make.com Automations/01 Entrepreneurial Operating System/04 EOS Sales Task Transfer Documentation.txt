Sales Task Transfer Scenario Documentation
Purpose:
This scenario automates the process of transferring sales tasks from one department to another within an Airtable database. It listens for incoming webhooks, retrieves task details, and based on the specified department, it creates a new record in the appropriate department's table while deleting the original task record.
